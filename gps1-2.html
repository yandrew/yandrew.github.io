What is pair programming?

Pair programming is a system of collaborative programming where one person drives and one person navigates.

Why important to DBC?

It's important to the culture of DBC. Everybody at DBC is a teacher and a student. It teaches you to collaborate and work with all different types of people.

Driver vs Navigator

Driver: Mainly the person that types the code. They focus on the current state of the code.

Navigator: They look at the bigger picture of the code. The navigator plans direction the code and driver will go.

Reflection

It was our 4th pairing and second time pairing together. Things went pretty smoothly between us. From the start, we already knew how both worked. We both had similar level of skill in HTML/ CSS. We both filled each other gaps of knowledge at times. We ran into a problem of position. We talked through the problem thoroughly and eventually solved it together.
